Everything You Need to Manage Your Herd

SAIOMS is designed for real Indian field conditions: harsh light, mixed breeds, offline villages, and real-world governance. Technology should feel trustworthy — like a well-run dairy, not a flashy app.

AI Breed Identification

Ensemble CNN model combining MobileNetV3, EfficientNet-B0, and ResNet-50 identifies 50+ Indian indigenous breeds including Gir, Sahiwal, Murrah, and Red Sindhi with 96.5% accuracy in under 150ms.

Secure QR Ownership

AES-256 encrypted QR codes store complete ownership history, vaccination records, and breed data. Tamper-proof, scannable even when partially damaged, works offline.

Real-Time Health Tracking

Automated vaccination reminders, breeding cycle alerts, and veterinary visit scheduling. Predictive analytics detect disease outbreaks before they spread.

Offline-First Mobile App

Flutter app works in zero-connectivity zones. Captures images, classifies breeds on-device, stores data locally, and auto-syncs when connection returns. Supports 12 regional languages.

Cloud-Scale Backend

AWS/GCP hybrid infrastructure handles 10,000 transactions/second. Firestore NoSQL database, Apache Spark analytics, auto-scaling handles monsoon-season surges.

Fraud Prevention

Blockchain-ready ownership ledger reduces fraudulent resale by 30–40%. Multi-party approval for ownership transfers. Full audit trail for government compliance and NADRS integration.

How It Works — From Photo to Proof

A simple workflow that respects ground reality: low bandwidth, mixed lighting, and the need for trust. One photo. One QR. Full traceability. Jai Kisaan.

1 Capture

Capture Animal Photo

Open the SAIOMS app, point camera at the animal. YOLOv8 auto-detects and crops the animal region. CLAHE enhancement corrects for rural lighting conditions.

2 Identify

AI Identifies Breed

The ensemble CNN model processes the image through MobileNetV3, EfficientNet-B0, and ResNet-50 in parallel. Weighted voting produces breed prediction with confidence score.

3 Encrypt

Generate Encrypted QR

Owner details, breed classification, vaccination history, and unique animal ID are encrypted with AES-256 and embedded in a QR code that survives 30% damage.

4 Manage

Track & Manage

Dashboard shows health timeline, alerts for upcoming vaccinations, ownership transfer requests, and AI-predicted health risks based on regional disease data.
